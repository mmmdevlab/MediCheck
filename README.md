# MediCheck

**MediCheck** is a collaborative appointment management platform that creates an accountability safety net between patients and their trusted caregivers.

**How It Works:**

**For Patients:**

- Track all medical appointments in one place
- Log daily health status with one-tap emoji system
- Create action items from doctor visits
- Request help from assigned caregivers when needed
- Maintain control over who can see their schedule

**For Caregivers:**

- View appointments of patients who've granted access
- See patient's latest health status (without medical details)
- Receive and respond to support requests
- Monitor patient's task completion
- Provide accountability without privacy invasion

## The Core Innovation:

Traditional appointment apps are single-player. **MediCheck is multiplayer healthcare coordination** - connecting patients who need support with caregivers who want to help, while respecting medical privacy.
